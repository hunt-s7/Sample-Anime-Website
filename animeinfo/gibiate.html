ANIME INFO
GIBIATE
Type: Summer 2020 Anime
Plot Summary: In 2030, Japan. A virus has infected humans throughout the world. Infected people turn into different forms of monsters based on their ages, sexes, and races. The virus is named "Gibia"—after being rich in variety like gibia. Just then, a pair of samurai and ninja appeared in such a blighted wasteland of Japan. They both traveled from the early Edo period, fighting together with help from a doctor who tries to find cure for Gibia. Facing ceaseless attacks from Gibias and outlaws that attack travelers for food, they start the dangerous journey with enemies all around.
Genre: Action, Fantasy, Horror, Martial Arts, Samurai
Released: 2020
Status: Ongoing
Other Names: ジビエート
